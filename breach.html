Biography
"Breach, the bionic Swede, fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. The damage and disruption he inflicts ensures no fight is ever fair."

Breach's main gimmick is the ability to use all of his abilities through the geometry of the map, whether it is through walls, roofs, or terrain. He can send an Aftershock to blow up enemy cover and Flashpoints to blind all enemies. Using powerful kinetic blast, Breach can create Seismic Blasts, which dazes all enemies hit.
