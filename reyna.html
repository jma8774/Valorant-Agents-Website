Biography
"Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat, popping off with each kill she scores. Her capability is only limited by her raw skill, making her highly dependent on performance."

Reyna has an aggressive, and duel-centric playstyle. Reyna's unique mechanic are the Soul Orbs, which drop when she kills an opponent and which, upon consumption, give her various bonuses â€” from massive healing to invisibility. Reyna's ultimate ability is Empress, which turns her into a rapid-fire death machine, dramatically increasing all her combat stats. Every kill renews Empress' duration, and a skilled Reyna player can wipe away the entire team in one swift offense.
