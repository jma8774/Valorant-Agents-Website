Biography
"The genius of Germany. Killjoy secures the battlefield with ease using her arsenal of inventions. If the damage from her gear doesn't stop her enemies, her robots' debuff will help make short work of them."

Killjoy is a sentinel who makes use of her many inventions in battle. She can set up a Turret and an Alarmbot for security surveillance to ensure no enemies are around. Using her grenades and Lockdown device, she can cut off enemy movement if they are around.
